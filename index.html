Crystal Collector

Here's how you play the game


  1. there are four crystals
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  2. Here's How the app works:
  3. There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page
  4. The player will be shown a random number at the start of the game.
  5. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.
  6. The player will not see the exact amount of points per crystal unless they click on a crystal
  7. Once a crystal is clicked on the player's score will update
  8. The player wins if the total score if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game.
  9. The player loses if their score goes above the random number.
  10. The game will restart if the player wins or loses.
  11. When the game begins again, the player should see a new random number, it is not required to hit the refresh page as a means to restart the game. If you do it will not keep your values for wins and losses.
